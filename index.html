Proposal Summary

Six Features.
One Seamless Experience.

ThreadPath is a holistic UX upgrade for Weaves — not individual features but a complete, interconnected customer journey designed to increase engagement, reduce friction, and build lasting loyalty.

01 / DISCOVERY

Smart Fabric Finder

A guided 3-step quiz that replaces endless browsing with a personalised, curated shortlist. Reduces decision fatigue and increases purchase confidence for first-time customers.

02 / INSPECTION

Texture Explorer

An interactive zoom magnifier that lets customers inspect weave structure at detail impossible in physical retail. Closes the tactile gap of online fabric shopping.

03 / CURATION

Colour Story Navigation

Replace colour filters with narrative-driven colour stories. Customers explore moods and aesthetics, not hex codes — creating an emotional connection to the collection.

04 / CONFIDENCE

Enhanced Product Detail

Tabbed product pages with fabric story, care instructions, and artisan origin. Built to give customers the full context they need to justify a premium purchase.

05 / RETENTION

Occasion Tagging & Reorder

Post-purchase occasion tagging creates a smart purchase history that enables timely, personalised re-engagement — turning seasonal shoppers into year-round customers.

06 / CONVERSION

Streamlined Checkout

A distraction-free, mobile-optimised checkout with smart address recall and flexible local payment options — designed specifically for the Pakistani e-commerce customer.
